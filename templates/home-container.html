The Surf Club at USF is a community of students passionate about surfing. It is open to USF students of all ages and skill levels. The club offers members a wide variety of opportunities to engage with their fellow students and the Florida surfing community. Whether you have never touched a surfboard or have been surfing all of your life, the Surf Club at USF seeks to enrich your college experience.
